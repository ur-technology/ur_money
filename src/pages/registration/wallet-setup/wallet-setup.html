Step 4 of 4 - Secret Phrase Setup

Choose a 5 to 10 word secret phrase using only lowercase letters and numbers.

Make sure you remember your secret phrase or keep a record of it because you will need it to make transactions later. We don't store your secret phrase, and it can never be recovered if you lose it. For maximum security, try to pick a string of words that has never appeared in print or online, and that no one would guess.
